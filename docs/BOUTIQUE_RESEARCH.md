## DATA VERIFICATION FRAMEWORK (Forensic Approach)

### Principle: Apply Mathematical Scrutiny to All Revenue Estimates

**Verification Formula**:
```
Monthly Revenue Estimate (EGP) =
(Monthly Traffic) × (Conversion Rate) × (Average Basket Size)
```

**Example - Boutique with 50K/month traffic**:
```
50,000 users × 1.5% conversion × 400 EGP = 300,000 EGP/month (~$6K USD)
```

**This framework ensures**:
- Currency clarity (EGP vs USD)
- Time period verification (monthly vs annual)
- Realistic benchmarking across competitors
- Basis for A7la Diva (now Hex-Diva) positioning
