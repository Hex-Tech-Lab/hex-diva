## CRITICAL INSIGHT: REVENUE DATA SCRUTINY

**Finding**: StoreInspect's "$2M–$10M/month" for Catwa Deals is likely inflated.

**Verification Formula**: Monthly Revenue (EGP) = Traffic × Conversion Rate × Basket Size
