# Zik Analytics Research Plan: Validate Boutique Intelligence

**Purpose**: Use analytics to validate boutique research findings with actual revenue data  
**Timeline**: 3–4 hours for all 4 phases  
**Outcome**: Concrete revenue benchmarks + proven product margins + content strategy for Hex-Diva

---

## OVERVIEW: Analytics Methodology

Key Analytics Approach:
- Extract revenue data from competitor stores
- Calculate margins via supplier cost-matching
- Identify viral products using sales thresholds
- Map social strategy (Instagram reels, TikTok, etc.)
- Discover product niches via competitive analysis

**Key Insight**: Actual store data reveals metrics that manual research cannot capture.

---

## PHASE 1: Store Discovery

**Goal**: Identify 5–10 high-revenue cosmetics stores

**Steps**:
1. Search keywords:
   - `"cosmetics Egypt"`
   - `"beauty eyelashes"`
   - `"nails makeup"`
2. Filter settings:
   - Min revenue: $15K/month (signals viable niche)
   - Min products: 50+ SKUs
   - Created: 2023+ (newer, faster growth)
3. Sort by revenue (descending)
4. Shortlist top 5–8 stores
5. Export store list (domain, revenue estimate, traffic estimate)

**Expected Output**: 5–8 high-revenue stores identified

**Time**: 15 minutes

---

## PHASE 2: Revenue Benchmarking

**Goal**: Get revenue data for shortlisted stores

**Steps** (per store):
1. Record:
   - Last 30-day revenue (USD)
   - Sales count (total units)
   - Average Order Value (AOV)
   - Installed apps
   - Social links
   - Ad platforms used
   - Shopify plan

**Verification Checklist**:
- Does revenue match conversion formula?
- Is currency USD or EGP?
- Does traffic match estimates?

**Time**: 5–10 minutes per store × 5–8 = 40–80 minutes

---

## PHASE 3: Product Profitability

**Goal**: Identify profitable products (not just bestsellers)

**Steps** (for top 20–30 products):
1. Identify best-selling products
2. Find AliExpress supplier cost
3. Record retail price
4. Calculate gross margin
5. Filter products meeting:
   - 12+ sales in 30 days (proven demand)
   - 60%+ gross margin
   - High review count
   - Multiple variants

**Expected Output**: 15–25 validated products with margin data

**Time**: 3–5 minutes per product × 20–30 = 60–150 minutes

---

## PHASE 4: Social Strategy

**Goal**: Identify which content drives traffic + conversions

**Steps** (for top 2–3 competitors):
1. Visit Instagram/TikTok
2. Analyze top viral posts
3. Record per post:
   - Content type (lifestyle, demo, UGC, tutorial, etc.)
   - View count
   - Engagement rate
   - Product presence (% of frame)
   - CTA type
   - Hashtags
   - Audio/sound
   - Upload date

**Expected Output**: 
- 5–10 viral post case studies per competitor
- Content patterns identified
- Posting frequency optimal range

**Time**: 20–30 minutes per competitor × 2–3 = 40–90 minutes
