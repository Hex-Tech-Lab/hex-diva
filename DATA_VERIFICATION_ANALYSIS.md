# Data Verification Analysis: Egyptian Cosmetics E-Commerce Revenue Estimates
**Date**: 2026-07-09  
**Purpose**: Validate StoreInspect figures and boutique revenue estimates using mathematical forensics  
**Approach**: Conversion rate × Traffic × Basket size → Estimated monthly revenue (EGP)
---
## CRITICAL FINDING: StoreInspect Data Requires Scrutiny
### Original Claim vs Reality
**StoreInspect Report (Catwa Deals)**:
- Reported: $2M–$10M/month revenue
**Red Flag Analysis**:
- $2M USD/month = $24M USD/year
- If true: Catwa Deals would be ~$1.2B–$5.2B USD annual (unrealistic for single Egyptian cosmetics store)
- More likely: Figure is annual revenue, or in Egyptian Pounds, or transaction volume not profit
---
## PROPOSED VERIFICATION METHODOLOGY
### Formula: Monthly Revenue (EGP) = Traffic × Conversion Rate × Average Basket Size
Traffic (users/month)
↓ × Conversion Rate (0.5%–3%)
↓ × Basket Size (EGP)
↓
Monthly Revenue (EGP)
↓ ÷ 51 (exchange rate)
↓
Monthly Revenue (USD)

---
## CASE STUDY: Catwa Deals (StoreInspect: 200K–1M traffic/month)
### Scenario A: Conservative Estimate (Low Traffic)
**Assumptions**:
- Monthly traffic: 200K users
- Conversion rate: 1% (conservative for e-commerce)
- Average basket size: 400 EGP (2–3 items × 150–200 EGP avg)
**Calculation**:
200,000 users × 1% × 400 EGP = 800,000 EGP/month
800,000 EGP ÷ 51 = $15,686 USD/month ($188K USD/year)

**Realistic Check**: ✅ Plausible for established cosmetics store
---
### Scenario B: Moderate Estimate (Mid Traffic)
**Assumptions**:
- Monthly traffic: 500K users
- Conversion rate: 1.5% (typical for cosmetics e-commerce)
- Average basket size: 450 EGP (3 items × 150 EGP avg)
**Calculation**:
500,000 users × 1.5% × 450 EGP = 3,375,000 EGP/month
3,375,000 EGP ÷ 51 = $66,176 USD/month ($794K USD/year)

**Realistic Check**: ✅ High-performing store, achievable
---
### Scenario C: Optimistic Estimate (High Traffic)
**Assumptions**:
- Monthly traffic: 1M users
- Conversion rate: 2% (upper range, quality traffic)
- Average basket size: 500 EGP (3–4 items × 150–170 EGP avg)
**Calculation**:
1,000,000 users × 2% × 500 EGP = 10,000,000 EGP/month
10,000,000 EGP ÷ 51 = $196,078 USD/month ($2.35M USD/year)

**Realistic Check**: ⚠️ Very high for single Egyptian store, but possible if multi-channel
